Welcome!

I am a Senior Research Engineer (Web Standards Technologist) at Samsung Research UK. I work on web privacy, verifiable credentials, and secure systems. Also, I am associate in W3C Technical Architecture Group (TAG). My career is rooted in bridging academic research, standardisation, and real-world deployment.

My previous research projects have published in major security conferences and journal. I have been featured in esteemed media outlets such as the Economist, Wall Street Journal and The Guardian). I hold two US patents (I and II).
