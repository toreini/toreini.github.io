Ehsan Toreini@Durham University

Assitant Professor in Security Engineering

I am assistant professor (lecturer) in computer science at Durham University.The main theme of my research is security engineering and practical cryptography. I research on physical security, web security and trustworthy machine learning; however, I am always ready to explore exciting research topics. My research have been published in esteemed media outlets such as The Economist, Wall Street Journal, Guardian and BBC.

Research Interests: Physical Security and authentication, Integrity Assurance Mechanisms, Side Channel Attacks, Web Browser Security, Trustworthy Machine Learning, Trust in Emerging Technologies

Also, I am interested into hardware security and secure identification of smart devices in various Internet of Things (IoT) infrastructures.

Student project? If you're a Durham University student looking for a final undergrad project or MSc thesis projects, please refer to this page.
Work with me? If you are looking for a PhD or Postdoc, please send an email to me with your proposal and CV.
